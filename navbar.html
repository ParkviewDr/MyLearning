I met Nicole in late August of 2006 at LaSalle University. The freshmen cross country team was invited over the girl's teams' house for board games. That was the night I awkwardly said hello to Nicole for the first time. Over the next few years, our relationship grew primarily through road trips to see or compete for the team at meets up and down the East Coast. It wouldn't be until 2011 that we started dating. That fall, we moved into our first apartment in York, PA. Once again, we would strengthen our relationship through road trips, many road trips. We moved more than a handful of times in 2012. It was an adventure and we didn't know where we would end up, but we loved it. In 2013, we settled down in King of Prussia. In February of 2015, with the help of Maille O'Dea, I picked out a ring and proposed to Nicole. We picked the date of September 3rd, 2016 which happens be ten years since we first met.
